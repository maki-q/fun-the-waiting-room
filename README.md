# The Waiting Room

For when you've been on TikTok too much and need a cleanse.

A minimalist browser game about time perception. No clocks. No timers. No progress bars. Just you and your internal sense of how long a second really is.

## How to Play

1. Each round tells you to wait a specific number of seconds (10s, 30s, 45s, 60s, 90s)
2. There are no visual clues - no ticking clock, no countdown, no animations
3. When you think the target time has passed, press the button
4. The game reveals how long you actually waited and scores your accuracy
5. After five rounds, you get your overall rating

Can you feel 90 seconds pass without any reference? Most people can't.

## The Concept

Time perception is remarkably unreliable. Studies show that boredom, anxiety, body temperature, and even whether you're having fun all distort your sense of duration. The Waiting Room strips away every external reference and forces you to rely purely on your internal clock.

The environment is deliberately minimal - a dark, quiet screen with almost nothing to look at. Like an actual waiting room, the emptiness is the point.
